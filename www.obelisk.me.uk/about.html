About this Site

This site is the personal work of Andrew Jacobs. All the material was been lovingly researched and turned into fine tuned HTML by yours truly.

I have no objection to people copying sections of the website to their local hard disk for personal use but anyone I catch republishing my work will be hung up by their short and curlies.

Why did you remove some of the pages?

I decided a few months ago that I was probably sharing a little too much on these pages so its been cut back to the sections that most visitors seem to like.

Why do you have advertising?

I have personally born all the costs of running this web-site for the last 15 years. It's become a teenager and I thought it was old enough to start dipping it's hand into its own pocket and re-embursing me for at least a portion of the monthly hosting costs.

I'm giving Google Adsense a trial. I don't expect to be recieving $5000 cheques in the post anytime soon but its interesting to see how many people come to the site on a daily basis.
